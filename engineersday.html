Event Description: Engineers Day by ISTE

Engineers' Day by the ISTE Club celebrates engineers' contributions and fosters innovation. Observed on September 15th, honoring Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, the event features:

  • Competitions: Hackathons, coding challenges, and robotics.
  • Workshops: Sessions on emerging technologies and career growth.
  • Guest Lectures: Insights from industry experts and alumni.
  • Exhibitions: Innovative student projects and models.
  • Activities: Quizzes, debates, and team-building games.
  • Awards: Recognizing excellence in engineering and education.
