Treasure Hunt

Event Description: Treasure Hunt by Mathletes Club

The Mathletes Club organized an engaging and intellectually stimulating event called Treasure Hunt. This event was designed to combine problem-solving skills with teamwork and a sense of adventure. Here's how it unfolded:

Team Formation:
Participants were grouped into teams of 5 members each. Teamwork and collaboration were key to succeeding in this event.

Solving Questions:
Each team was presented with a series of challenging mathematical and logical questions. Successfully solving these questions was the gateway to advancing in the treasure hunt.

Location Puzzle:
For every question solved, the team earned a slip revealing a location. However, the twist was that the location was given in the form of a puzzle. Teams had to decipher the puzzle to identify the next location.

Hunting the Treasures:
At each location, unique shapes were hidden. Teams had to find these shapes as evidence of their success in decoding the puzzle.

Winning Criteria:
The team that found the most locations and collected the highest number of shapes within the given time frame was declared the winner.

This exciting event tested participants' analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and collaborative skills, making it a memorable and fun-filled experience for all. The blend of mathematics and adventure truly embodied the spirit of the Mathletes Club!
