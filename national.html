National Science Day

Event Description: National Science Day by ISTE Club

National Science Day, celebrated annually to honor Sir C.V. Raman's discovery of the Raman Effect, is organized by the ISTE Club to inspire scientific curiosity and innovation.

Key highlights include:

  • Guest Lectures: Insights from experts on emerging scientific trends.
  • Workshops & Seminars: Hands-on sessions on advanced topics.
  • Exhibitions & Competitions: Showcasing innovations, quizzes, debates, and more.
  • Awareness Programs: Promoting science's role in everyday life and careers in STEM.

The event fosters scientific temper and encourages students to contribute to technological progress.
